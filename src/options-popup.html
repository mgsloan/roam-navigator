Usage

Navigation mode is initiated by pressing Alt+g, or just g when not editing text. Then just type the visual hints to navigate!

To open links in the sidebar, hold Shift while typing the last letter of the visual navigation key.

The key for toggling the sidebar is a backtick (`).
